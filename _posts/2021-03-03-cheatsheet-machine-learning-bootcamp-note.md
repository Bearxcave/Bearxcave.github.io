### Linear regression

Data is often split into 3 sets:

* Training data
* Validation data
* Test data

Make clear about your classification metrics:

* Accuracy
* Recall
* Precision
* F1-Score : $2 \frac{\mathrm{precision}*\mathrm{recall}}{\mathrm{precision}+\mathrm{recall}}$
